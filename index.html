Sasaima Birdwatching Route — Creation Plan

The “Sasaima Birdwatching Route” was designed as a local initiative to strengthen sustainable tourism. The project was carried out in three key phases focused on understanding, planning, and promoting the birdwatching experience in Sasaima.

Step 1: Recognition

Identification of the most representative bird species, natural sites, and communities interested in participating. This stage established the ecological and cultural foundation of the route.

Step 2: Design

Structuring of the trails, logistics, and interpretive elements. Maps, signage, and local guides were coordinated to connect biodiversity with responsible tourism.

Step 3: Promotion

Creation of digital and educational materials to showcase the route to national and international visitors, positioning Sasaima as a reference point for avitourism in Cundinamarca.
