Ecological Trails

Choose a route, enjoy mid-Andean forests, traditional villages and photographic stops.

Panche Trail

Ancient route crossing mid-Andean forest with caves, waterfalls and cultural stories — ideal for curious hikers and photographers.

Acuapal – Guayacundo

A long route through villages, colonial stone paths and coffee farms — a real immersion into rural life.

Jardín del Agua

A small eco-park in Santa Teresa: guided hikes, river bathing, coffee tours and yoga sessions in nature.

Waterfalls & Natural Pools

Cool down in hidden waterfalls, follow tunnels and discover natural pools surrounded by cloud forest.

Wellness Tourism

Yoga at sunrise, guided meditation, mindful trekking and ancestral natural baths — spaces to disconnect and restore.

Outdoor Yoga

Sunrise or sunset sessions with panoramic mountain views (example: Finca Albacerrada).

Natural Baths

Ravines and wells along the Guane Trail are perfect for ancestral hydrotherapy and cooling off after hikes.

Local Therapies

Workshops using coffee & cocoa oils, herbal infusions, and reflexology offered at small local farms.
